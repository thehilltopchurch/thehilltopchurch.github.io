Be Our Partner

Join Us In Spreading The Gospel Worldwide.


Partnership goes beyond simple collaboration - it's a divine vision. By partnering with us, you become part of a mission to bring God's presence and message to people and nations worldwide.

The Partnership Bridge:


Ecclesiastes 4:9 teaches that partnership is a powerful relationship where two or more people share responsibilty to achieve a common goal. Philippians 4:15(CEV) highlights the shared blessings of partnership. Just as the church at Philippi supported Paul and received blessings in return, your support for this ministry will help spread the good news and bring blessings into your life.

What Makes You A Partner?


Partners are individuals, families, businesses and other ministries that faithfully and periodically sow some level of financial support into Hilltop Church International. Your partner gifts enable HCI to carry out its mission and position you to receive the rewards for the work, the annointings of the ministry and a harvest on your seed sown.


Join Us today in this mission. Together, we can make an eternal impact.

Become A Partner

Take The Gospel Farther And Deeper

At Hilltop Church International, we are dedicated to connecting people worldwide with God through the transformative message of the Gospel. Everyday, livess are changed as individuals experience God's love and learn how to love in return - His way. When you contribute to this ministry, you help us:


  • Spread the message of God's boundless love and grace via digital platforms, books, and by continually upgrading and maintaining media equipment and websites for more impactful outreach.
  • Equip and raise disciples, sowing into their communities and feeding them both physically and spiritually.
