What We Believe

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible contains the inspired Word of God. It is the only written revelation from God to man and is profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for training in righteousness. See 2 Timothy 3:16.





Salvation

We believe that Jesus died for our sins. We affirm that salvation is only attained through faith in the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ. Additionally, we hold the belief that He bestows His Holy Spirit of promise upon all who receive salvation through faith in Christ Jesus. See Romans 5:1; 5:8-9, Ephesians 1:13-14, Galatians 4:6, Acts 5:32, and Acts 2:38.





The Church & her mission

The Church is the body of Christ (1 Corinthians 12:12-27), embodying a threefold mission: to spread the message of Christ worldwide as His ambassadors (Acts 1:8; Matthew 28:18-20; John 17:8), to regularly gather in fellowship for worshiping God (1 Corinthians 12:13, Hebrews 10:25), and to equip God's people for ministry (Ephesians 4:11-16; 1 Corinthians 12:28, 14:12).

A Message

AK and I express sincere gratitude for the meaningful connection we’ve forged with you. We view our encounter as a privilege and hold it in high regard. It’s an opportunity to share the transformative impact of His power and grace with you. Our purpose is to teach the gospel truth to the nations, with a particular focus on His unconditional love, grace, and faith. Your involvement in this ministry is welcomed, and we hope it marks the commencement of a relationship that deepens your understanding of our benevolent heavenly Father. Blessings to you,

Eddie & Akosua Koomson
