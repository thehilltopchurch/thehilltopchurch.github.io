Welcome to the Hilltop Church

We're delighted that you've chosen to check us out. We are a church family and a community shaped by our mission, firmly believing that the essence of the Church lies in a people who wholeheartedly love God, know their identity in Christ, and show compassionate love to their neighbors.

Our Belief
Our Vision

Hilltop Church Int. is a global ministry with the vision of reaching the nations with the gospel of Christ while teaching and equipping believers for effective ministry in an atmosphere of love, fellowship, faith, and power.


Our Mission

Our mission is to extend the reach of the Gospel of Christ to nations, tribes, tongues, and people. Together, we embark on a transformative journey, delving deeper into our identity in Christ, uncovering the blessings we possess in Him, and realizing our purpose in Him.

Our Family

Our commitment is to grow as a family by cultivating godly fellowship, engaging in impactful worship, embracing deep discipleship, participating in broader ministry, and making a significant outreach in sharing our faith.


Our Message

Our message centers on Christ's sacrificial death for our sins, His burial, and His resurrection in accordance with the Scriptures. Furthermore, through the Holy Spirit, this resurrection imparts new life to all who believe.
